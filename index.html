Investment Opportunity

19955 Ashwood Drive Land Partition & Sale | Bend, Oregon

Part 1: Project Overview

Project Summary

This project focuses on the acquisition and partition of a prime 0.89-acre lot at 19955 Ashwood Drive in Bend's desirable Wood River Village. Our plan is to purchase the parent lot, demolish the existing manufactured home, and then partition the property into two new, buildable lots for resale. This project is projected to conclude within a 12-15 month timeframe, capitalizing on the strong local demand for premium residential land.

Location, Location, Location

Wood River Village is one of Bend's most sought-after neighborhoods, valued for its established character and incredible location. The property is just moments away from the Deschutes River, the bustling Old Mill District, and numerous parks and trails. This prime positioning significantly enhances the value and marketability of the new lots, making them highly attractive to builders and individuals looking to create a custom home in a premier Bend location.

The Strategic Plan

1

Acquire Parent Lot

Our partnership will purchase the entire 0.89-acre property and demolish the existing, non-conforming mobile home.

2

Partition Lot

We will then work with the city, county, and utility providers to legally divide the property into two new, buildable lots.

3

Sell Lots

Finally, we will list and sell the two "shovel-ready" lots on the open market, targeting local builders and individuals eager to build.
