Project Summary

This project focuses on acquiring a prime lot at 19955 Ashwood Drive, partitioning it, and selling the resulting parcels. Our plan is to purchase the parent lot, demolish the existing mobile home, and then divide the property into two new, buildable lots. Our partnership will then sell these two lots on the open market, capitalizing on the strong local demand for premium residential land.

Location, Location, Location

This property is located in a highly desirable Bend neighborhood, valued for its established character and incredible location. The property is just moments away from the Deschutes River, the bustling Old Mill District, and numerous parks and trails. This prime positioning significantly enhances the value and marketability of the new lots, making them highly attractive to builders and individuals looking to create a custom home in a premier Bend location.
