Investment Opportunity

19955 Ashwood Drive Land Partition & Sale | Bend, Oregon

Part 1: Project Overview

Project Summary

This project focuses on the acquisition and partition of a prime 0.47-acre lot at 19955 Ashwood Drive in Bend's desirable Wood River Village. Our plan is to purchase the parent lot, demolish the existing manufactured home, and then partition the property into two new, buildable lots for resale. This project is projected to conclude within a 12-15 month timeframe, capitalizing on the strong local demand for premium residential land.

Location, Location, Location

Wood River Village is one of Bend's most sought-after neighborhoods, valued for its established character and incredible location. The property is just moments away from the Deschutes River, the bustling Old Mill District, and numerous parks and trails. This prime positioning significantly enhances the value and marketability of the new lots, making them highly attractive to builders and individuals looking to create a custom home in a premier Bend location.

The Strategic Plan

1

Acquire Parent Lot

Purchase the entire 0.47-acre property at 19955 Ashwood Drive and demolish the existing mobile home to prepare for development.

2

Partition Lot

Work with the city, county, utility providers, and contractors to legally divide the property into two distinct, buildable residential lots.

3

Sell Two Lots

List and sell the two newly created, shovel-ready lots on the open market to builders or individuals.

Navigating Project Complexity

While the "acquire-partition-sell" strategy is straightforward, the execution is intricate and requires experienced management. This project involves many moving parts, from the demolition of the existing home to the complex installation of all-new, separate utility services (water, sewer, power) for two lots where there was previously only one. Navigating the city's permitting and approval process for a partition is a significant undertaking in itself.

To meet these challenges, I bring direct, hands-on experience. I have personally managed two similar partition projects within Wood River Village. I also have a ready network of professional contractors to ensure the project is completed efficiently and correctly.
